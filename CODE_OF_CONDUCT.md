# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project is a small, friendly, open-source effort. Everyone interacting in
the project's spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions — is expected to be
respectful, considerate, and constructive, regardless of background or
experience level. The goal is simple: keep this a welcoming place to build and
learn.

## Standards

Be kind. Assume good faith. Critique ideas, not people. Harassment,
discrimination, personal attacks, and other unwelcome conduct are not
tolerated.

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1, as its full standard of behavior. Please read it for the complete
expectations and the list of unacceptable behavior.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer at
**c.archer.simmons@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and handled
discreetly. The maintainer may remove, edit, or reject contributions and
comments that violate this code, and may ban any contributor for behavior
deemed inappropriate.
